Bobby's Guessing Game

Guessing Game Instructions

  • Guess a number from 1-100 and click the "Submit" button
  • After each guess, you will be given an indicator of how close you are
  • Also, a picture of a New York Jets player will appear with the number you guessed
  • You are allowed a total of 5 guesses before you lose
  • If you click the "Give up" button, the answer will be provided to you
  • Start over at any time by clicking the "Start over" button
